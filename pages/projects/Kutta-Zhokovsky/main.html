Conformal transformations and the Kutta-Zhukovsky airfoil


If you wait a bit, a desmos graph demonstrating the Kutta-Zhukovsky airfoil will load. The dotted lines are streamlines, and the blue indicates the pressure. As you can see, there are higher pressure below the wing and lower pressure above it, leading to lift. I have imposed the Kutta condition, which requires that the air leaving the back of the airfoil do so smoothly. You can play with parameters, though I would recommend turning off the pressure before you do so to decrease loading times (that can be found under "Bernoulli's principle").


Eventually, I plan on adding more detail here about how all of this is derived from the complex potential and conformal transformations.
